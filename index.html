Welcome to Phil O'Reilly's Music Studio!

Hello all. My name is Phil O’Reilly. I am a 31 year old Chicago based singer-songwriter, guitarist, and vocalist. I have lead and been involved in choirs and bands for nearly my whole life. Last year, I made the big decision to quit my current job and pursue music full time. Part of that dream was wanting to start my own music studio. So for the last year I’ve been teaching several students and looking to expand and help more people find their voice and hopefully follow their own dreams!
